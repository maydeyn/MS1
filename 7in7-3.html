Question

How to raise awareness of vision impairment? How can normal sighted people engage with vision impaired population?

Conceptualization

I was very intrigued by the mobile app Be My Eyes, it connects vision-impaired people with sighted volunteers for visual assistance through a live video call. I loved the idea of the volunteer-driven, community-oriented platform that connects people.

Development

I appreciate that most museums nowadays offer audio support (both available on-site and online) for their artworks. However, I believe you can take the museum experience to the next step. This will be an interactive virtual museum website that allows people to explore all the museums around the world. Users can contribute to enhance the experience for the visually impaired by submitting audio recordings to the virtual museum space:
  • Descriptions of artwork or interior of the museum
  • Related fun facts, interesting findings, or even personal stories
After a user make submissions, other users, museum moderators can upvote the comments they found interesting.
    Additional features
  • 360-degree view of various museums around the world.
  • All user inputs will be audio recordings.
  • Integrating speech recognition that allows users interact verbally with the virtual museum
Keywords: community, interaction, awareness, audio

Outcome


Reflection

    What I liked about this prototype:
  • It is accessible on most devices and it is straightforward, easy to use
  • It is a community-driven platform that allows people from around the world explore museums and share their thoughts.
    • Issues/future development:
  • Some users are not comfortable with using audio to interact with their machines and/or having their voice recorded.
  • Develop packages for each museum that users can download and explore offline.
