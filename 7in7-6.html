Question

How to create an experience that encourages the use of other senses?

Conceptualization

One of the most popular party games in China: Werewolf (also known as Mafia) is loved by many, the concept of using strategy, logic, deduction, psychology and memory. I want to revolutionize this classic game and create another level of complexity by limiting the use of vision, but allowing users to touch.

Keywords: textured material, touch, memorization

Development

I started the process by brainstorming some of the most recognizable textures to correspond with the roles in the game:
  • Civilian - Sand
  • Witch - Velvet
  • Prophet - Metallic
  • Werewolf - Faux Fur

I will create the cards using these materials. Players will need to figure out their roles based on touching the textures.
