Question

How to raise awareness about the prevention of potential visual impairment? How to promote good eye health, including encouraging regular visits to eye doctors?

Conceptualization

I came up with this idea of an installation piece inspired by park poets. However, it will be a modified phoropter (an instrument commonly used during an eye examination) writes the poem instead of a human.
Keywords: park poet, AI, phoropter

Development

Poetry Through the Windows to the Soul
  1. Users will look into the phoropter machine and have their eyes scanned.
  2. The machine will analyze your eyes' condition, color, emotions.
  3. Then it will write a short poem and print it out for you.

Outcome

Reflection

    What I liked about this prototype:
  • Interesting approach to repurpose an eye exam machine, combining the element of science with literature
  • Remind people about taking care of their eyes through this experience.
    • Issues/future development:
  • More modification on the aesthetics of phoropter, making it even less intimidating.
  • Make the machine/AI able to diagnose some basic eye conditions and translate that information into poetic language.
