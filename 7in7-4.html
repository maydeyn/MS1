Question

How to raise awareness about the prevention of potential visual impairment? How to promote good eye health, including encouraging regular visits to eye doctors?

Conceptualization

I came up with this idea of an installation piece inspired by park poets. However, it will be a modified phoropter (an instrument commonly used during an eye examination) writes the poem instead of a human. The audience can look into the machine, then scan your eyes, analyze it and generate a poem based on your eye condition, color, and analyze your emotions all just through your eyes.
Keywords: park poet, AI, phoropter
